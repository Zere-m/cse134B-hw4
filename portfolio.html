Motivation Behind the Project:

This project was work of a team of 5 people taking CSE 110 - Software Engineering at UCSD. It was inspired by the need to provide a user-friendly platform for individuals to track their health and fitness goals effectively.

Lessons Learned

Throughout this project, I learned about the complexities of state management in React and the importance of user experience design in software enginnering.
